About the Application

With the rising popularity of superhero movies, there has been a heightened awareness of superheroes in our midst. The frequency of superhero (and supervillain) sightings is increasing at an alarming rate. This app is designed to keep track of all the organizations and sightings of the superheroes and villains. It also displays all the locations they have been spotted as well as a list of all the heroes and villains.
